Simple Past Tense

1. The Simple Past Tense

The Simple Past Tense is used to describe actions, events, or situations that happened at a specific time in the past. It is one of the fundamental tenses in English and is used to narrate past activities, experiences, or states.

2. Uses of the Simple Past

2.1 Completed Actions:

Describes actions that were completed in the past. Examples:

She visited Paris last year.

He finished his homework.

2.2 Series of Actions:

Describes a series of actions that occurred in the past. Examples:

She woke up, brushed her teeth, and had breakfast.

He went to the store, bought some milk, and came home.

2.3 Past Habits:

Indicates habitual or repeated actions in the past. Examples:

They always walked to school.

He often played soccer in the park.

2.4 Specific Time in the Past:

Refers to actions that occurred at a specific time. Examples:

The concert started at 8 PM.

She called me yesterday."

2.5 States and Conditions:

Describes states or conditions that existed in the past. Examples:

"She was very happy."

"The weather was cold last week."


3. Signal Words

Yesterday

Indicates an action that happened the previous day. "I saw her yesterday.

Last week/month/year

Refers to a time period in the past. "He traveled to Japan last year.

Ago

Refers to a specific time in the past. "They moved here five years ago.

In 2010 (any specific year)

Indicates an action that took place in a specific year. "She graduated in 2010.

When

Often used in questions or statements to refer to past actions. "When did you arrive?

This morning/afternoon/evening (if it's now later in the day)

Refers to a time earlier in the current day. "I met him this morning.

5. Spelling of Verbs in the Simple Past

Spelling Rule Base Form (Infinitive) Simple Past
Regular Verbs: Add -ed play played
Verbs ending in -e: Add -d like liked
Verbs ending in consonant + y: Change -y to -ied carry carried
Verbs ending in vowel + y: Add -ed play played
Verbs ending in a single consonant preceded by a single vowel: Double the consonant and add -ed stop stopped
Irregular Verbs: Varied forms (refer to list) go went

6. Questions in Simple Past Tense

She ________________ (visit) her friends last weekend.

They ________________ (enjoy) the concert on Saturday.

Maria ________________ (finish) her project yesterday.

The company ________________ (launch) a new product last month.

We ________________ (celebrate) his birthday with a big party.
